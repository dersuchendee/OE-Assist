The competency question "At what time did a certain actor start working for a specific theatre?" can be answered using the ontology provided. The ontology includes classes and properties related to actors, their employment, and the start date of their employment at a theatre. Here's a SPARQL query to extract the answer:

```sparql
SELECT ?actor ?theatre ?startDate
WHERE {
  ?actor a :Cl_Actor .
  ?actor :employedAt ?employment .
  ?employment a :Cl_Employment .
  ?employment :employmentIn ?theatre .
  ?employment :employmentStartDate ?startDate .
}
```
