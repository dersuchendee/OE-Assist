The competency question "What is the evaluation statement given by a certain doctor for a certain employee?" can be answered using the ontology provided. The ontology includes classes and properties that relate doctors to evaluations and evaluations to employees, as well as the evaluation statement itself.

Here is a SPARQL query to extract the answer:

```sparql
SELECT ?doctor ?employee ?evaluationStatement
WHERE {
  ?doctor a :Cl_Doctor .
  ?doctor :givesEvaluation ?evaluation .
  ?evaluation a :Cl_Evaluation .
  ?evaluation :givesEvaluation ?employee .
  ?evaluation :evaluationStatement ?evaluationStatement .
}
```
