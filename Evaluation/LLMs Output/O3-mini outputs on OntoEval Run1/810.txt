Below is an example SPARQL query that, under the closed‚Äêworld assumption, retrieves for each person the specific degree and its study period (using the reified degree record in Cl_PersonDegree):

PREFIX : <http://www.example.org/test#>
PREFIX rdfs: <http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#>
PREFIX xsd: <http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema#>

SELECT ?person ?personLabel ?degreeTitle ?studyStartDate ?studyEndDate
WHERE {
  ?person a :Cl_Person .
  OPTIONAL { ?person rdfs:label ?personLabel . }
  ?person :hasDegree ?personDegree .
  
  ?personDegree :studyStartDate ?studyStartDate ;
                :studyEndDate ?studyEndDate ;
                :degreeType ?degree .
  ?degree :degreeTitle ?degreeTitle .
}
ORDER BY ?person ?studyStartDate
